# Modular Figma assets needed

## Export rules

- Do not export a full kiosk page as a low-resolution background. Full-screen exports in `public/assets/figma/reference/` are visual references only and are never rendered as stretched page backgrounds.
- Export character and decorative illustrations as transparent PNG at 2x or 3x resolution.
- Export stickers as transparent PNG at 2x or 3x resolution.
- Export vector logos and icons as SVG where possible, or as high-resolution transparent PNG.
- Keep final photobooth frame templates as 1200x1800 PNG files.
- QR codes must remain dynamically generated by the application and must not be exported from Figma.
